# Eric M Greeff


## Summary and Objective Statement
Experienced construction professional with a strong background in carpentry, truss design, and project management. Proven ability to lead teams, manage resources, and deliver high-quality results on time and within budget. Seeking a challenging position in the construction industry where I can utilize my skills and experience to contribute to the success of the organization.

---

## Door Shop Associate
*Star Building Materials, Calgary Alberta*
### Core Responsibilities
- **Fabrication & Assembly**: Cutting, shaping, and assembling doors, frames, and trim in a shop environment.
- **Hardware Installation:** Installing hinges, locks, handles, closers, and other hardware to specifications.
- **Measurement & Precision:** Measuring doorways and materials accurately to ensure proper fit and operation.
- **Maintenance & Repair:** Troubleshooting and repairing existing doors, including automatic or overhead systems.
- **Customer Service:** Assisting customers, providing product advice, and maintaining a professional demeanor.
- **Safety & Cleanliness:** Adhering to safety protocols, operating machinery safely, and maintaining a clean work area.

## Finishing Carpenter/Insulator
*Intercontinental Truck Body, Coaldale Alberta*
### Core Responsibilities
- **Installation:** Installing baseboards, crown moulding, window trim, casing, stairs, and custom cabinetry.
- **Precision Work:** Hanging interior/exterior doors, fitting hardware, and ensuring accurate, polished finishes.
- **Measurement & Planning:** Reading, interpreting, and working from blueprints and architectural drawings.
- **Material Prep:** Cutting, shaping, and joining materials, including hardwoods and trim, using hand and power tools.
- **Quality Control:** Inspecting work to ensure compliance with standards, often repairing or adjusting, and performing fine-sanding or finishing.

## Truss Designer
*Highwood Trusses, High River Alberta*
### Core Responsibilities
- Utilize MiTek software to create accurate roof and floor truss designs and layouts.
- Ensure all designs comply with local building codes and structural requirements.
- Optimize designs to minimize material and labor costs while ensuring structural integrity.
- Produce detailed design drawings, takeoffs, and production documents.

## Foreman
*Startek Enterprises, Vulcan Alberta* 
### Core Responsibilities
- **Site Supervision:** Oversee daily operations, assigning tasks and providing leadership to laborers, apprentices, and subcontractors.
- **Safety Compliance:** Ensure strict adherence to safety protocols (e.g., OSHA, PPE) and conduct regular site inspections to maintain a secure environment.
- **Scheduling & Coordination:** Manage project timelines, adjusting daily schedules for weather, equipment issues, or material delays.
- **Quality Control:** Inspect completed work to ensure it meets quality standards, specifications, and project plans.
- **Resource Management:** Requisition materials, supplies, and equipment, ensuring tools are maintained and available.
- **Personnel Management:** Hire, train, mentor, and, when necessary, discipline crew members.

## Lead Hand Carpenter 
*Nascor Trusses*
### Core Responsibilities
- **Assembly:** Fabricate wood trusses by placing, securing, and fastening components (lumber and metal plates) according to engineering drawings and work orders.
- **Tool & Machine Operation:** Use hand tools, pneumatic nail guns, and overhead cranes to move and secure materials.
- **Quality Control:** Inspect finished trusses for defects, accuracy, and adherence to specifications.
- **Machine Operation:** Operate gantry presses to embed connector plates and run finished products through the final press.
- **Production & Safety:** Meet production deadlines, maintain a clean workspace, and follow all safety protocols.
- **Handling:** Move finished trusses to the yard for shipping.
